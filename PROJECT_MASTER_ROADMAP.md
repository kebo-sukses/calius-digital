### 🎨 Writing Style Guidelines: Human, Not AI

#### ❌ AI Writing (AVOID)
```
"It's important to note that landing pages are crucial for conversion optimization. 
Moreover, they provide significant benefits for digital marketing campaigns."
```

#### ✅ Human Writing (USE THIS)
```
"Pernah nggak sih, udah pasang iklan ratusan ribu per hari tapi pengunjung datang, 
lihat-lihat sebentar, terus langsung kabur? Nah, bisa jadi masalahnya bukan di produk 
atau harga. Tapi karena halaman yang dikunjungi pengunjung bukan landing page yang bener."
```

#### Natural Writing Characteristics:
- ✅ Use "gue/Anda" (not formal "saya/kami")
- ✅ Rhetorical questions: "Pernah nggak sih...", "Tau gak..."
- ✅ Daily analogies: "Landing page itu kayak sales yang fokus..."
- ✅ Short paragraphs (4-5 lines max)
- ✅ Strategic emojis (not excessive): 💡, 🚀, ✅, 📊
- ✅ Highlight with bold/italic naturally
- ✅ Stories/anecdotes in case studies
- ✅ Concrete data: "340% increase", "9.7% average"
- ✅ Conversational transitions: "Gini deh, bayangin..."
- ✅ Direct address: "Anda pasti pernah...", "Coba deh perhatikan..."

## 💡 PRO TIPS & BEST PRACTICES

### 📝 Content Creation
1. **Quality > Quantity**
   - 1 epic article/week > 5 mediocre articles
   - Focus on depth, not just word count

2. **Write for Humans First, SEO Second**
   - Natural conversational tone
   - Answer real user questions
   - Use examples and stories

3. **Update Regularly**
   - Refresh articles every 6 months
   - Update statistics and data
   - Add new case studies

### 🔗 Link Building
1. **Start Early**
   - Don't wait for traffic to build links
   - Begin outreach in Month 1

2. **Focus on Quality**
   - 1 DR50 link > 10 DR10 links
   - Relevance matters more than quantity

3. **Diversify Sources**
   - Guest posts
   - Directory submissions
   - Social signals
   - Forum/community participation

### 📊 Analytics
1. **Check Data Daily**
   - First thing in morning: GSC + GA4
   - 5-10 minutes review
   - Note any anomalies

2. **Act on Insights**
   - High bounce rate → Improve intro
   - Low CTR → Better meta description
   - Position 11-15 → Add backlinks

3. **Document Everything**
   - Keep record of changes
   - Note what worked vs what didn't
   - Build playbook over time
