Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I download my purchased template? +

After completing your purchase, you'll receive an email with a download link. You can also access your downloads from your account dashboard. The download link is valid for 1 year and allows up to 5 downloads.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied? +

Yes! We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with your purchase for any reason, contact us within 30 days for a full refund. No questions asked.

Do I get lifetime updates? +

Absolutely! All our templates come with lifetime updates at no additional cost. Whenever we release new features, improvements, or security patches, you'll get them for free.

Can I use the template for multiple websites? +

Each license allows you to use the template on one website. If you need to use it on multiple sites, you'll need to purchase additional licenses. We offer volume discounts for multiple purchases.

What kind of support do you provide? +

We provide comprehensive support including email support, live chat, detailed documentation, and video tutorials. Our support team is available Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM GMT+7.

Are the templates mobile-friendly? +

Yes! All our templates are built with a mobile-first approach and are fully responsive. They look perfect on all devices including smartphones, tablets, and desktops.
